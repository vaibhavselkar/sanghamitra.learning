Health and Medicine Vocabulary

Level 1 Level 2 Level 3 Level 4 Level 5

Health

/hɛlθ/
noun
Definition: the state of being free from illness or injury.
Synonyms: wellness fitness vitality
Antonyms: illness disease sickness
Examples:
  • Regular exercise contributes to good health.
  • Mental health is as important as physical health.

Medicine

/ˈmɛdɪsɪn/
noun
Definition: the science or practice of the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of disease.
Synonyms: medication drug remedy
Antonyms: disease illness
Examples:
  • Advances in medicine have increased life expectancy.
  • She studies medicine at the university.

Therapy

/ˈθɛrəpi/
noun
Definition: treatment intended to relieve or heal a disorder.
Synonyms: treatment remedy cure
Antonyms: harm injury
Examples:
  • She is undergoing therapy for her anxiety.
  • Physical therapy helps in the recovery of injured patients.

Diagnosis

/ˌdaɪəɡˈnoʊsɪs/
noun
Definition: the identification of the nature of an illness or other problem by examination of the symptoms.
Synonyms: identification determination recognition
Antonyms: misdiagnosis
Examples:
  • The doctor made a quick diagnosis based on the symptoms.
  • An accurate diagnosis is crucial for effective treatment.

Prevention

/prɪˈvɛnʃən/
noun
Definition: the action of stopping something from happening or arising.
Synonyms: avoidance protection precaution
Antonyms: promotion encouragement
Examples:
  • Vaccination is an effective method of disease prevention.
  • Regular exercise and a healthy diet can help in the prevention of heart disease.

Nutrition

/nuˈtrɪʃən/
noun
Definition: the process of providing or obtaining the food necessary for health and growth.
Synonyms: nourishment diet food
Antonyms: starvation malnutrition
Examples:
  • Good nutrition is essential for maintaining a healthy body.
  • She studied nutrition to become a dietitian.

Immunity

/ɪˈmjunɪti/
noun
Definition: the ability of an organism to resist a particular infection or toxin by the action of specific antibodies or sensitized white blood cells.
Synonyms: resistance protection defense
Antonyms: vulnerability susceptibility
