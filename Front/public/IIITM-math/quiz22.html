📐 Differentiability Formatting Guide

How to format your answers:

  • Derivatives: f'(x)=2x, dy/dx = 3x²
  • Differentiability conditions: differentiable at x=0, not differentiable
  • Limit definition: lim_{h→0} (f(x+h)-f(x))/h
  • Continuity & differentiability: continuous but not differentiable
  • Points of non-differentiability: corner point, cusp, vertical tangent
  • Theorem names: Rolle's Theorem, Mean Value Theorem

Differentiability Examples:

f(x)=|x| at x=0 → continuous but not differentiable (corner)
f(x)=x² sin(1/x) for x≠0, f(0)=0 → differentiable at 0
f(x)=x^{1/3} → vertical tangent at x=0, not differentiable
