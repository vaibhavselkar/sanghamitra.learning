ℹ️ Answer Formatting Guide

How to format your answers:

  • Exponential Functions: e^x = $e^x$, a^b = $a^b$, exp(x) = $\exp(x)$
  • Logarithms: log(x) = $\log(x)$, ln(x) = $\ln(x)$, log_a(b) = $\log_a(b)$
  • Math Symbols: inf = ∞, sqrt(x) = √x, ^ = exponent
  • Fractions: 1/2 = $\frac{1}{2}$, (x+1)/(x-1) = $\frac{x+1}{x-1}$
  • Equations: x = -1/2, e^3 (multiple answers with commas)
  • Intervals: (-inf, 3] = $(-\infty, 3]$

Quick Examples for Exponential Functions:

e^(2x) → $e^{2x}$
2^(x+1) → $2^{x+1}$
log_10(100) → $\log_{10}(100)$
f(x) = 3e^(-x/2) → $f(x) = 3e^{-x/2}$
domain: (-inf, inf) → $(-\infty, \infty)$
