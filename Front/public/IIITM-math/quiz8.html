📐 Exponential Function Guide

Formatting Examples:

  • e^x or exp(x) - Natural exponential
  • 2^x, 3^(x+1) - Exponential with base
  • e^(2x) - Exponential with coefficient
  • ln(x), log(x) - Natural log
  • log10(x) - Base 10 logarithm

Properties: e^(a+b) = e^a × e^b, ln(e) = 1
