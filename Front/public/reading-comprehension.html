Reading Comprehension


Reading comprehension is the ability to understand, interpret, and analyze written texts. It involves not just reading the words on a page but also connecting them to meaning, context, and one’s own knowledge and experiences. This skill is crucial as it allows readers to grasp the main ideas, draw inferences, and make logical conclusions from what they read.

Main Idea

Understanding the central concept or primary message of the passage.
Author's Purpose

Identifying the reason why the author wrote the passage.

Inference

Drawing conclusions based on evidence and reasoning beyond the text.

Specific Details

Locating and understanding specific pieces of information in the passage.

Title of the Passage

Choosing or understanding an appropriate title that reflects the passage's content.

Vocabulary Context

Understanding word meanings based on how they are used in the passage.
